## STATE LICENSING SUMMARY (NEW TRADES)

### Trades with STRONG state licensing (exam prep opportunity):
- **Fire Sprinkler** - ~42 states require license + NICET certs
- **Elevator Mechanic** - ~45 states require license + QEI cert
- **Low Voltage/Fire Alarm** - ~45 states require license + NICET certs

### Trades with certification-based licensing:
- **Lineman** - Union apprenticeship, no state license typically
- **Ironworker** - NCCCO Rigger certification valued
- **Diesel Mechanic** - ASE T-series certifications
- **Millwright** - Vibration analyst, alignment certs
- **Tile Setter** - CTI certification
- **Appliance Repair** - EPA 608 required for refrigerants

### Trades with minimal licensing:
- **Glazier** - C-17 in CA, few others
- **Insulator** - Union certification
- **Drywall** - C-9 in CA, few others
- **Cabinet Maker** - C-6 in CA, few others
- **Pipefitter** - ~20 states (often under plumbing)
